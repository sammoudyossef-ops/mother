    Mind & emotions

    Pregnancy can bring joy, fear, tenderness, anger, doubt, and all of them can be valid in the same day. You don’t need to be perfectly calm to be a good mother.

    Private emotional note
    Grounding protocol
    1. Name it“I am feeling ___, and it makes sense because ___.”
    2. Ask the body“Do I need food, water, rest, movement, or reassurance?”
    3. Ask for one thingOne clear request beats silent resentment.
    4. Come back to safetySlow breath, hand on belly, remind yourself: “One moment at a time.”

    Medical & admin

    Keep appointments, questions, and documents in one calm place. This app gives organization, not medical advice.

    In France, the first prenatal exam and pregnancy declaration are done before the end of the 3rd month. There are generally 7 prenatal consultations and 3 recommended ultrasounds. Confirm your exact schedule with your healthcare professional.
    CPAM / CAF checklist
    • Ask doctor/midwife to declare pregnancy online
    • If paper form: send CPAM + CAF copies before week 14 / end of 3rd month
    • Check Ameli account confirmation
    • Check CAF account / PAJE eligibility
    • Create folder: ultrasound, blood tests, maternity documents, mutuelle

    Baby prep

    Prepare without panic. You do not need everything today. You need clarity, timing, and a few safe essentials.

    Essentials checklist
    • Car seat — new only, safety critical
    • Crib mattress — new only
    • Crib / baby bed — second-hand okay if safe
    • 0–3 month clothes — second-hand is perfect
    • Diapers, cotton, changing supplies
    • Hospital bag ready by week 36
    • Baby corner / room ready by November
